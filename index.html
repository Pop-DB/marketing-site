What is PopDB?

PopDB is a vibe coding platform. We provide you with an app builder, database, hosting, auth, storage, and integrations. Everything you need to build and deploy apps without stitching together multiple services.

How does pricing work?

PopDB has one simple monthly price that covers unlimited apps and agents. No per-request fees, no surprise bills. You get predictable costs so you can focus on building.

Do I need DevOps experience?

No. PopDB is designed to be used by people who cannot code.

Can I migrate my existing app to PopDB?

Yes. PopDB supports standard PostgreSQL databases, so you can import your existing data. Our desktop app makes migration straightforward.

What kind of support do you offer?

We offer documentation, community support, and direct assistance. Whether you're stuck on setup or scaling, we're here to help.

Do I have to use the PopDB app builder?

No. You can use the CLI, VS Code, Claude, or your own workflow.
